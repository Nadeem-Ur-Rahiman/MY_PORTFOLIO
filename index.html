About Me

The story behind the code.

Some engineers learn to code. I learned to think in systems. Born and raised in Chirala, Andhra Pradesh, I arrived at Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education with a perfect 10 GPA and a quiet conviction: technology should solve the problems that genuinely matter.

"I don't wait for the future. I build it — one model, one deployment, one paper at a time."

My projects are not academic exercises. The cardiac disease prediction system I built uses Explainable AI because I believe a patient deserves to understand their own diagnosis, in their own language. The New Parent App — now peer-reviewed and published in IEEE — was designed for Indian parents in low-resource settings, because the tools that exist were not built with them in mind.

At the BNP Paribas Hackathon 2025, my team's real-time market sentiment dashboard earned Finalist recognition. At Smart India Hackathon 2024, our Team AEROBYTES tackled autonomous drone delivery for medical emergencies in remote India. At ANVESHANA'25, I reached National Round 3 with a sub-₹799 Smart Key Tracker prototype at BMS Institute, Bengaluru.

I completed a Java internship at VaultofCodes (AICTE Certified), hold certifications from Oracle, HackerRank, and NPTEL, and am graduating in 2026 — already well beyond my year.
